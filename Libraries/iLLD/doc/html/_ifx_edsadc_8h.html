enum IfxEdsadc_AnalogMux

Allows copying of bitfield AMX into bitfield TIMESTAMP.

Enumerator
IfxEdsadc_AnalogMux_doNotCopyAmx 

Do not copy, timestamp uses all 16 bits.

IfxEdsadc_AnalogMux_copyAmx 

Copy AMX to bits TIMESTAMP[15:14], timestamp uses lower 14 bits.
